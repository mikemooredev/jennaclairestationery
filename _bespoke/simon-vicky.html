Vicky and Simon got married in Manchester at the Radisson Blu Edwardian in November 2015. They wanted to go for a woodland theme. I translated this in kraft printed invitations paired with hand drawn woodland animals and finished with a rafia tie to hold all the elements together. This gave the rustic feel they were after to contrast the modern clean feel of the venue. Each table on their wedding day were named after different woodland animals and were decorated with pieces of tree trunks and pine cones to bring to life the woodland feel.

VENUE: RADISSON BLU EDWARDIAN, MANCHESTER
STATIONERY: JENNA CLAIRE STATIONERY
