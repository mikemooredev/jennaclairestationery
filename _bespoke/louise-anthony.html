Louise and Anthony got married at the stunningly beautiful Dorfold Hall in Cheshire, in May 2019. The bride just so happens to be one of my best friends so being asked to create something special for their wedding was an honour. She has a slight obsession with gold and wanted to get this colour into every aspect of their wedding so, along with getting to draw their wonderful venue, I got to work designing something special. Roses were also something that the couple wanted to incorporate due to the grooms surname, and eventually the brides, surname being ‘Rose”. I worked on a small hand painted bespoke rose floral design to incorporate into their stationery. Their on the day stationery then incorporated the venue drawing and rose design, as well as lots of gold!
