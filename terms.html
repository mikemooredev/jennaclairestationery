Terms & Conditions

PROOFS
It is important that proofs are carefully checked over before proceeding to print. You are responsible for the proof reading of all digital PDF proofs sent to you and printing will not commence until you have confirmed you are happy to proceed. You will be liable for the cost of re-printing due to any errors discovered after printing that appear on the proofs. You will be able to make 2 lots of changes to your design. Any further revisions will be charged at £30 per revision.

Please note: Although all products displayed are as accurate as possible for colour, the colour you see will depend on your screen and printed colours will always differ very slightly to that of a computer screen. In most cases a sample can be sent beforehand.

TIMEFRAMES
The timeframe of your order can take anything between 2 to 8 weeks depending on the level of customisation required to your stationery, the amount of revisions required and your response times to proofs that are sent. It is important to consider that design revisions and/or delays in feedback may affect the timeline and potentially delay your order.

Once I have all the required information from you and have receipt of the deposit, I will get started on your designs. An electronic proof of your design will be sent over to you. Once you have given the final approval to print and final payment has been received, your stationery will be printed and dispatched.

I always endeavour to complete orders as quickly as possible but will never compromise on the love and care they deserve. On the rare occasion that I require to extend these dates, you will be notified immediately.

PAYMENT
Before any design work commences a 50% deposit will be required. The remaining payment is due on final approval prior to printing.

PUBLICATION
Any images of work designed by Jenna Claire Stationery must be correctly credited.
