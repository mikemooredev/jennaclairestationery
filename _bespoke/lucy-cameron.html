Lucy and Cameron got married at Wilderness Reserve, Suffolk in July 2017, with a weekend full of activities and celebrations. They came to me with lots of ideas that they wanted to incorporate into their wedding stationery. Their brief was clean, fresh, charming, elegant, and sophisticated invitations, but to include an element of fun. They wanted to somehow incorporate penguins and vegetables, with a colour scheme of gold, herby greens and creams. After a skype call between myself and the bride, who lives in New York, it was my job to design and deliver the vision. Who would have thought you would see penguins walking through a vegetable patch? The bespoke motif was used throughout their invitations and on the day stationery, and was even made into a rubber stamp and used for a snap chat filter!
