A little about me

“Every great love starts with a great story” NICHOLAS SPARKS

I’m Jenna Claire, a creative and passionate designer with a love for stationery.
And then suddenly, you meet the one that makes you forget about yesterday, and dream about tomorrow.

For me love is one of life’s greatest feelings and the idea of being involved with couples during such a special time in their lives prompted the creation of my brand.

Jenna Claire is a brand designing beautiful stationery and paper goods for all occasions.
I love to create unique and personal designs that reflect just what your wedding day is all about. Whether you have your own vision, or just need a little guidance, together we can
create the perfect collection that can be enjoyed, shared and treasured for years to come.

Most paper products are printed in house and hand crafted with the love they deserve!
